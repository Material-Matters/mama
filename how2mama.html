WELCOME !

This is our official guide. The aim of this page is to give you as much information as possible to help you open your own material bank, inside your Universities or wherever you like. The page is divided in chapters: by following them step 2 step you will be able to set up successfully your material bank. You can also use the buttons to skip to the part you have interest on.
